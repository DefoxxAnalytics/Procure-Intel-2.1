Tail Spend Characteristics

Typical Tail Spend Profile

  • 80% of suppliers contribute only 20% of spend
  • High transaction costs (avg. $150-250 per PO)
  • Limited contract coverage (<30%)
  • Fragmented across 100+ categories
  • Minimal volume discounts
  • No strategic supplier relationships

Optimized Tail Spend Profile

  • Consolidated to 20-30% of original suppliers
  • Reduced transaction costs (<$50 per PO)
  • Increased contract coverage (>70%)
  • Focused on 20-30 key categories
  • Volume-based pricing agreements
  • Preferred vendor partnerships
