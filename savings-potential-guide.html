Best Practices

Key considerations for successful implementation and achieving projected savings.

Data Quality Considerations

  • Completeness: Ensure all spend data is captured
  • Accuracy: Validate vendor names and amounts
  • Categorization: Proper category assignment is crucial
  • Time Period: Use 12-month rolling data for accuracy

Stakeholder Communication

  • Present conservative estimates to executives
  • Use realistic estimates for planning
  • Share optimistic scenarios to inspire teams
  • Always include implementation timelines

Success Factors

  • Executive Sponsorship: Critical for strategic initiatives
  • Change Management: Address resistance proactively
  • Quick Wins: Build momentum with early successes
  • Measurement: Track actual vs. projected savings

Common Pitfalls to Avoid

  • Over-promising on savings potential
  • Underestimating implementation effort
  • Ignoring change management needs
  • Focusing only on price reduction
  • Neglecting supplier relationships
